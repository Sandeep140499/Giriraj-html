    About Us

    Founded in 1993, Giriraj Foils Pvt Ltd (GFPL) offers a comprehensive portfolio of very high-security films, stamping foils and laminates used by Authentication, Security Printing, Packaging, Healthcare and Decorative Industries for Brand, Revenue and Consumer Protection applications. Our 200+ professional combined experience, craft, innovation and ambition has earned GFPL an irreproachable reputation defining the contours of the ‘High-Security Films / Foils Industry’ in India and globally. For the last 30 years, our ethics, commitment and business values have led us to maintain leadership as a critical supplier providing the high-security film market to the industry in the Indian arena
